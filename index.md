## About Ken Jones Photography

**Ken Jones Photography** is a New York City photography and video production studio located on Fulton Street in Manhattan’s Financial District. The studio works with executives, corporate teams, agencies, fashion brands, media companies, entrepreneurs, designers, and creative professionals who need strong visual content created with professional lighting, direction, and production support.

Ken Jones has more than 30 years of experience creating corporate headshots, executive portraits, commercial photography, fashion editorials, beauty images, product photography, interview videos, and branded visual content for clients throughout New York City and beyond.
